The Land of Beauty

Know about Sinai


Sinai Peninsula in Egypt is a famous tourist destination in the country. The town is referred as a perfect destination to enjoy the sun, sea and adventures. Sinai houses the most popular as well as least developed beach resorts of Egypt, offering travelers of every kind an ideal accommodation option. Sinai also enjoys fame as being the scuba diving center offering many other activities like canyons and mountains for trekking, rock climbing, mountain biking, camping and even room for indulging in desert dune buggies and safari adventures.

Interesting tourist sites of Sinai


Sinai is blessed with many tourist attractions, helping every visitor experience an amazing holiday here. Just few such places of tourist interest include;


Blue Hole

Touted to be the most beautiful diving spot in Sinai, this is often referred as The Canyon, and it gets its name from the breath-taking, spectacularly clear waters that are blue in color.


Colored Canyon

The Colored Canyon is a famous safari destination which is usually combined with a tour to the St. Catherine’s Monastery. The place offers a breathtaking view of amazing rock formations and canyon walls. The place is among the must-see attractions of Sinai where you can escape to experience silence and beauty at its best.


Dahab

Dahab meaning gold, is an ideal place where wind surfing can be enjoyed to the fullest. The place is adorned with palm groves adding ample beauty to the coast. Reaching Dahab is much easy and this is a place if looking for tourist activities in Sinai.


Blue Desert

Located close to St. Catherine Monastery, it was in 1980-81, that a Belgian artist Jean Verame painted many boulders blue with the permission of late President Sadat, as a symbol of end of war between Egypt and Israel. The place is an extraordinary one and worth paying a visit to.


Mount Sinai

Touted to be a biblical peak, the place has a religious significance to Islam as it’s believed to be where Boraq, Mohammed’s horse ascended to heaven. The place must be visited by every tourist coming to Sinai.
